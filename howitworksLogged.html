How It Works

We are a group of environmentalist. Our objective is to encourage people to save natural and other resources through reuse of things. Each product takes a lot of raw materials, man-hours and energy before it comes to your hand. Per capita consumption is getting higher day by day. People around the world are using thousands of commodities for their living. If you observe, many of them are underutilized, grab our attention for very shorter period, be it toys, electronics accessories, fashion items, cloths etc etc.

So, if we don’t worship our store in the corner of our house, these things have no utilities after some time except some memories and illogical emotions. Now, how do you think if this product meet the need of someone who is living in your community or locality. Of course, you will happy to donate your unused things that has higher value and usability to others.

So we urge you to donate your unused items ( be it…) and support to fulfill other needs at the same time contribute to environment by saving resource. As you know, each product takes a lot resource before it is built in a factory or cottage.
